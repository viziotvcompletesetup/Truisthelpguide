Complete Activation Guide

Card activation is an essential step that allows you to start using your card for transactions. Without activation, your card cannot be used for payments, withdrawals, or online purchases.

The activation process is designed to be simple and secure. Users are required to enter their card details and verify their identity to ensure safety. This helps prevent unauthorized access and keeps your account protected.

Always make sure you are using a secure internet connection and avoid entering your details on unknown websites. Double-check your information before submitting to avoid any errors or delays.
